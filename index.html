    Facilities


    Operation Theatre

    The Operation Theatre department pride themselves on delivering quality care in a clean environment, delivered by teams with a vast range of expertise, for each specialty. It is equipped with a work station.


    Ventilator Facility

    Our programs are designed for patients who need short-term or continuous ventilator treatment and rehabilitation. Our ventilator teams are focused on taking an aggressive, multi-disciplinary approach to ventilator rehabilitation.


    24x7 Ambulance Services

    We are just a call away from you. Emergency department functioning 24hrs with competent doctors and nurses and also available ambulance facility.


    Digital X-Ray

    We will be providing the top-notch services of x-ray service. we take to ensure you receive the highest quality care and accurate results. We perform services to identify and troubleshoot the root cause of performance issues and errors.


    C-Arm

    X-Ray & C-Arm Solutions help the doctors to view the inside of the body without making any incisions.We are dedicated to the safe and efficient use of advanced imaging to help improve the health of our patients.


    Intensive Care Unit

    Our Intensive Care Units cater to patients suffering with severe and life-threatening illnesses and injuries, which require constant, close monitoring and support from specialist and medications in order to ensure speedy recovery and normal bodily functions.


    Central Oxygen

    Breathing is a complex process that relies heavily on the coordinated action of the muscles of respiration and the control center in the brain. We are equipped with central oxygen facility.


    Multipara Monitor

    High-capability, multi-function monitors are equipped in hospital to ensure a high-level of quality patient care. Patient monitors measure, record, distribute and display combinations of biometric values such as heart rate, SPO2, blood pressure, temperature and more.

    Specialities

    The Department of Emergency at Starwings Hospital is resolute in improving the standards of pre-hospital and emergency department services in all avenues possible, with a mission to achieve excellence as a regional and national leader in the field of emergency medicine.

    Emergency department provides initial treatment for a wide range of conditions like road traffic accidents, burns, poisoning, bites and stings, respiratory emergencies, cardiac emergencies like heart attacks, and stroke for both adult and pediatric age groups and is a one-stop facility with a full spectrum of services.

    The Department of Neuroscience offers comprehensive evaluation for management of patients with neurological disorders.

    The Centre provides Diagnosis and Comprehensive care to people with Neurological Disorders such as Headache Migraines, Back pain and Neck pain, Dizziness, Weakness, Numbness, Memory Loss, Head Injury, Acute Head & Spine Injuries, Sleep Neurology, Insomnia, Excessive Daytime Sleepiness, Snoring, Disturbed Sleep.

    The Orthopedic Department has skilled doctors who provide treatment for various disabilities.

    Joint pain, Fractures, Muscular pain, Strain, Sprain, Arthritis, Scoliosis, Back pain, Spondylosis, Osteoporosis, Disc Prolapse, Road traffic Accidents, Vertebral column dysfunction and many more.
    Treatments such as Knee Replacement surgery, Hip Replacement and Articular Surface Replacement, Shoulder Replacement and Revision surgery, Spinal surgery and spinal deformity correction surgery, Arthroscopic Surgeries, Disc excision and Disc Replacement and many more.

    General Medicine or Internal Medicine

    As your internal medicine provider, we hope to provide long term comprehensive care for both common & complex chronic ailments, in addition to providing a one stop solution for acute illnesses.
    We take time in getting to know you more during your first visit, in terms of your lifestyle and healthcare background. We assess the need for preventive care and individualize a treatment plan for your current conditions.
    We may also refer you to a specialist when the need arises for a specialized test or a distinct treatment. We are here to co-ordinate your care and will make sure all your health concerns are addressed in a timely manner.

    The department of Pulmonology deals with diseases of the lungs and the bronchial tubes, usually involving the evaluation of the upper respiratory tract (nose, pharynx and throat).

    The department offers expertise in diagnosis and management of diseases such as asthma, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD)

    General Surgery:

    General Surgery focuses on surgical intervention for conditions which do not respond to conservative management. The surgeons perform procedures on all specialties. For any advanced conditions, specialized intervention shall be carried out for the surgery.

    The department diagnoses and offer comprehensive treatment for various urological conditions.The centre is manned with a team of super specialists with a combined experience of several thousand cases..

    There are many urological conditions and diseases that can affect a person irrespective of their age and gender. Some of the most common urological conditions are Urinary-Tract Infectious diseases, Kidney stones, Benign Prostatic Hyperplasia(BPH), Renal disease and more.
    Urological Treatments: Doctors recommend some lifestyle changes to the patient in the initial stages to control the symptoms like limiting the amount of caffeine, quitting smoking, drinking enough water to stay hydrated. The medications may range from antibiotics to chemotherapy drugs depending on the type of urological condition.

    Our Doctors though readily treat the poly traumatic patients, recommend the Trauma Patients to undergo X-ray diagnosis of Trauma series to ascertain possible life threatening injuries.

    Polytrauma occurs when a person experiences injuries to multiple body parts and organ systems often, but not always, as a result of blast-related events.
    Polytrauma and multiple trauma are medical terms describing the condition of a person who has been subjected to multiple traumatic injuries, such as a serious head injury in addition to a serious burn.

    Our doctors carefully examine the X-ray report and deliver appropriate treatment to the patients.Poly Traumatic Patients are treated with utmost priority and urgency.
